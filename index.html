Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Wayfare

Is Wayfare free to use?

Wayfare offers a free plan with limited features, perfect for occasional travelers. For more advanced features like unlimited itineraries and real-time collaboration, check out our Pro and Business plans.

Can I share my itinerary with friends who don't use Wayfare?

Yes! You can share a view-only link of your itinerary with anyone, even if they don't have a Wayfare account. For collaborative editing, they would need to create a free account.

Does Wayfare work offline?

Offline access is available on our Business plan. This allows you to view and edit your itineraries even without an internet connection, with changes syncing when you're back online.

Can I import existing travel plans?

Absolutely! Wayfare supports importing from popular formats like PDF, Excel, and even email confirmations. Just forward your booking confirmations to your Wayfare account, and we'll organize them for you.
